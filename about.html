THE HEART OF YATHRI TOURS

Your Home of Legendary Sri Lankan Hospitality

Yathri Tours began with a simple vision: to share the authentic soul of Sri Lanka with the world. We don't just provide tours; we craft personalized experiences that blend the island's natural beauty with its centuries-old tradition of heartfelt hospitality.

  • Personalized Itineraries tailored to your soul
  • Expert Local Guides with deep ancestral knowledge
  • Sustainable & Responsible travel practices
  • Hand-picked luxury accommodations
OUR VISIONARY FOUNDER

The Legacy of Excellence

Inspiring Journeys Since 1973

"Hospitality is not a service, it's a way of life in Sri Lanka. At Yathri, we make sure every guest feels the warmth of our home on every step of their adventure."

Our founder's passion for the island's heritage and commitment to impeccable service laid the foundation for what Yathri Tours is today—a brand synonymous with trust, elegance, and discovery.
